Salmon stuffed burritos

Description

These delicious salmon stuffed burritos are prepared in no time, leaving you with all the time to enjoy them with your friends or family.

Enjoy these as lunch, an easy dinner or chop them up and serve as an appetizer while enjoying your favourite drink at the end of the day.

Ingredients

  • 400 gr fresh spinach
  • 250 gr boneless wild salmon fillet (frozen)
  • 223 gr Knorr "world dish" burritos
  • 100 ml water
  • 200 gr peas
  • 125 gr crème fraîche

You'll also need a low oven dish (20 by 30 cm)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 °C. Heat the spinach in a frying pan. Meanwhile, cut the salmon into cubes. Mix the spice mix from the package with the water.

  2. Stir the herb mix into the warm spinach. Add the salmon, stir and heat for 2 min. Add the garden peas and heat for 1 min. Mix the sauce mix from the packet with the crème fraiche and water to make a herb spread.

  3. Spread the tortillas from the package with half of the herb spread. Spread 3⁄4 of the spinach-salmon mixture on top. Roll the tortillas into burritos and place side by side in the baking dish. Spread the rest of the spinach-salmon mixture in a lane down the center of the rolls and spread the rest of the herb spread on top. Bake for about 10 min in the center of the oven.

This dish is for three people. If you want to eat it for four, serve tortilla chips and a sweet and sour cucumber salad alongside.
