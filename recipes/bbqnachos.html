BBQ nachos with pulled pork

Description

Looking for the ultimate BBQ snack? These mouth watering loaded nachos with pulled pork are irresistible. Fire up that BBQ and go!

Ingredients

  • 240 gr pulled pork
  • 200 gr natural felvoured tortilla chips
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 lime
  • half a head of a little gem
  • 3 table spoons of bottled topping cheese
  • 3 table spoons of bottled mango habanero topping

Steps

  1. Light the barbecue. Remove the plastic wrap and foil from the pulled pork and place the meat, with the liquid, in the aluminum bowl. Keep the sauce aside for a while. Roast the pulled pork on the barbecue for about 25 min. Halfway through the cooking time, spread the meat with the sauce from the package.

  2. Spread the tortilla chips on an aluminum tray and heat for the last 5-8 min. on the barbecue next to the pulled pork.

  3. Meanwhile, cut the onion into wafer-thin half rings, using a mandolin if necessary. Place in a bowl and squeeze the lime over it. Stir and let stand until use. Cut the lettuce into thin strips.

  4. Drain the liquid from the pulled pork and place in a small saucepan. Cook over medium-high heat for about 3 min until a thick sauce forms. Meanwhile, pull the pulled pork apart with 2 forks and mix the reduced sauce into the meat.

  5. Heat the cheese sauce in a saucepan 1-2 min. over medium-high heat. Divide the pulled pork, heated cheese sauce, mango habanero sauce, red onion and baby romaine lettuce among the tortilla chips and serve.
